About Me

👋 Hi,

My name is Akhil Bonthinayanivari, and I am a passionate Data Engineer with over 10 years of experience. I specialize in building scalable data pipelines, designing cloud-based ETL solutions, and working with technologies such as Python, SQL, Apache Spark, Snowflake, and Azure.

I am always eager to learn and grow as a developer, and I’m constantly seeking opportunities to apply my knowledge to help businesses optimize their data strategies.
